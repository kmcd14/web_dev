Our Story


Planted is the first vegan restaurant in Dundalk, Co.Louth! We opened our doors in April 2014.


We're a small and cozy vegan restaurant in the center of Dundalk. Our restaurant offers an international a la carte menu with dishes from all over the world both for lunch and dinner. And it is 100% vegan meaning everything is free from dairy, egg and meat. Reservations are highly recommended. And not just a day earlier - the earlier the better. It's never too early to book a table.
